## Mini-Audit Rules

- Keep it under 10 minutes per lead.
- Do not pretend public clues prove private workflow pain.
- Write the outreach as a question, not a pitch.
- Do not collect sensitive personal data.
- Do not scrape private profiles.
- Do not build custom solutions before a reply.
