# Opportunity Signals

Source: [[Scanner Overview]]

Use these as visible/manual clues when scoring leads. A signal is not proof. It is only a reason to ask a better question.

## Small Business Workflow Pain

- Outdated website
- No clear contact or booking flow
- Multiple scattered links
- Link in bio with messy tools
- Manual service menu with no form
- Public reviews mention slow responses
- Public reviews mention confusion about scheduling
- Business asks customers to text/DM for basic info
- No visible FAQ or intake process
- Repeated posts asking customers to message for details

## Public Small Business Signals

- "DM to book"
- No booking link
- No intake form
- No quote form
- Customer comments asking how to book
- Repeated questions in comments
- Link-in-bio with too many disconnected tools
- Outdated website
- No FAQ
- No follow-up or confirmation process visible
- Manual service menu
- Public reviews mentioning missed calls or slow responses
- "Text for price"
- "Message for availability"
- Google Form with unclear follow-up
- No clear CRM/intake process

## Booking/Intake Pain

- "DM to book"
- "Text to book"
- No intake form
- No quote request form
- No appointment scheduler
- Contact form asks for too little detail
- Booking requires back-and-forth messages
- Customer comments asking how to book
- Services listed without next-step instructions
- Public posts with manual availability updates

## Spreadsheet/Data Cleanup Pain

- Public spreadsheet
- Google Form feeding an unclear process
- Member/customer lists likely managed manually
- Dues/payment language without visible tracking process
- Manual signup lists
- Repeated event or booking lists
- Inventory/service menu updates done manually
- "Fill out this form and we will get back to you"
- Multiple forms for related workflows
- No visible dashboard/reporting process

## Lead Follow-Up Pain

- No follow-up call-to-action
- Many inquiries in comments
- Questions in comments not answered quickly
- "Message us for pricing"
- "DM for availability"
- No automated confirmation visible
- No lead magnet or capture flow
- No clear quote follow-up process
- Customer asks the same question repeatedly
- Business posts reminders manually

## Student Org/Frat Workflow Pain

- Google Form RSVP
- Linktree with multiple event/member/payment links
- Repeated event posts
- Manual payment/dues language
- Public dues/deadline reminders
- Public event attendance requirements
- Officer roles listed but no process clarity
- Recruitment/rush signups
- Philanthropy event signups
- Member list or chapter roster workflows
- Event follow-up not visible
- Check-in or attendance likely done manually
- GroupMe/Discord mentioned as the main coordination tool

## CS Student/Job Search Workflow Pain

- Applying to internships or new grad roles
- Talks about resume stress
- Asks for project/resume help
- Has projects but weak portfolio presentation
- Uses scattered docs/sheets for applications
- Posts about application chaos
- No clear project case studies
- No organized portfolio
- Repeatedly asks about tailoring resumes
- Tracks applications manually
- Interview prep notes scattered across tools
- Hiring/application chaos

## Rule

Use visible signals to choose better questions. Do not treat them as confirmed facts until the person replies.
