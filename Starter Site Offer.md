# Starter Site Offer

## Purpose

Secondary offer in the two-offer system for businesses with no website, N/A website, social-only presence, or weak web presence.

## Core Problem

They may be relying on calls, texts, DMs, referrals, or scattered posts instead of a simple website and lead intake system.

## Main Offer

Starter site + lead tracker.

## Packages

### $75 Website + Lead Flow Mini-Audit

- Review current public presence
- Identify lead capture gaps
- Recommend homepage structure
- Recommend quote/contact form fields
- Recommend follow-up tracker

### $150 Starter Landing Page

- 1-page website draft
- Services section
- Service area section
- Call-to-action section
- Quote/contact form mockup
- Basic FAQ
- Mobile-friendly layout

### $300 Landing Page + Lead Tracker

- 1-page website
- Quote/contact form
- Google Sheet lead tracker
- Follow-up status fields
- Simple SOP for responding to leads

### $500+ Website + Workflow Setup

- Multi-section site
- Lead intake form
- Lead tracker
- Follow-up templates
- Simple automation plan
- Handoff instructions

## Best Niches

- contractors/home services
- mobile detailers
- lawn care
- pressure washing
- handyman
- cleaning services
- local repair services
- personal trainers/coaches
- event vendors

## What Not To Claim

- Do not promise guaranteed leads
- Do not claim their current process is broken
- Do not say AI will fix everything
- Do not make private assumptions
- Do not imply internal systems were audited

## Best Outreach Angle

I noticed you may not have a simple website or quote flow set up. I made a quick starter mockup showing how customers could request a quote and how you could track follow-ups.

## Validation Rule

A mockup is not validation. Only replies, questions, calls, pilots, or payment interest count.
