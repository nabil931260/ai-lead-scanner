# Scanner Overview

## Purpose

The AI Lead Scanner is a tightly scoped local tool for finding, scoring, and preparing better outreach opportunities.

It exists to make manual outreach sharper, not to replace outreach.

## How It Fits

It can support a small service or portfolio project by turning manual lead research into a repeatable workflow:

1. Find businesses with public workflow or intake friction.
2. Score the opportunity with a consistent rubric.
3. Generate mini-audits and draft outreach for manual review.

It helps identify businesses and organizations that may have visible workflow pain before they are moved into a private outreach or validation tracker.

## Current Focus Update

Current active focus:

Public-market small business workflow opportunities.

Keep private contacts and warm outreach notes outside the public repository. This repo is intended for public-market research examples and reproducible tooling only.

Current goal:

Find public-facing businesses or niches with visible workflow problems and create mini-audits that can later become outreach, content, or service offers.

## What It Is

- Manual lead scorecard
- Lead input formatter for rough manually collected rows
- Mini-audit workflow
- Opportunity signal checklist
- Evidence-based scoring output
- Manual next-action queue categories
- Prompt library for evaluating public/manual lead information
- Test lead table
- Bridge from lead research to outreach

## What It Is Not

- Not a scraper
- Not a CRM
- Not a web app
- Not a SaaS
- Not a Chrome extension
- Not an automated DM system
- Not a lead database
- Not a replacement for talking to real people
- Not market proof by itself

## Allowed Inputs

- Private notes you are allowed to use
- Public pages reviewed manually
- Names/contacts manually pasted in
- Exported contact lists personally provided and permitted for use
- Public business/org pages accessible without login
- Your own observations about visible workflows

## Disallowed Inputs

- Private account scraping
- Login-gated data
- Automated DMs
- Hidden email extraction
- Private Instagram, LinkedIn, GroupMe, or Discord data
- Sensitive personal data
- Anything obtained by bypassing platform rules or access controls

## Outputs

- Scored lead list
- Visible workflow pain signals
- Evidence, pages checked, contact signals, and missing signals
- Mini-audit notes
- Offer angle
- Recommended message version
- Next manual action
- Qualified leads moved into a private outreach tracker

## 1-Day Build Rule

This scanner should take no more than one focused day to set up and start using.

If it takes more than 1 focused day before producing a ranked lead list and 3 ready-to-send messages, it has become procrastination.

## Validation Rule

This does not count as validation until real people reply.

A scored lead is not validation. A mini-audit is not validation. A prepared message is not validation.

Validation starts when someone describes a real workflow, pain point, workaround, or willingness to try/pay/give feedback.
