# Lead Scorecard

Source: [[Scanner Overview]]

## Scoring Categories

Score each lead out of 100.

| Category | Points | What To Check |
|---|---:|---|
| Likely pain level | 20 | Is there a clear sign they have a workflow, data, intake, follow-up, event, or job-search problem? |
| Ease of contacting | 10 | Can I contact them manually without scraping or private data? |
| Likelihood of reply | 15 | Are they warm, semi-warm, active, or likely to answer? |
| AI/workflow automation fit | 15 | Could a simple workflow, spreadsheet cleanup, mini-dashboard, or AI-assisted process help? |
| Revenue potential | 10 | Could this become paid work, a testimonial pilot, or useful proof? |
| Warmth of relationship | 10 | Do I know them, have a warm path, or share a community/context? |
| Evidence quality | 10 | Is the pain based on a real visible signal, public clue, or direct context rather than a guess? |
| Next best action clarity | 10 | Is it obvious what question/message to send next? |

## Score Interpretation

- **80-100:** message first
- **60-79:** good backup
- **40-59:** maybe later
- **Below 40:** ignore for now

## Public Market Lead Scoring Priority

For now, prioritize leads with:

- Visible workflow pain
- Easy public contact path
- Clear offer angle
- Simple manual/no-code fix
- Possible $75-$150 pilot
- Low privacy/regulatory risk
- Local/small enough to care

Deprioritize:

- Large companies
- Regulated medical/legal workflows
- Businesses with polished CRMs
- Businesses where the pain is not visible
- Businesses that would require scraping/private data
- Leads with no clear way to ask a low-pressure question

## Status Options

- Review
- Message first
- Backup
- Maybe later
- Ignore
- Moved to Outreach Tracker
- Waiting on reply
- Logged in Validation Tracker

## Confidence Levels

- **High:** multiple clear signals or direct context
- **Medium:** one clear signal plus reasonable fit
- **Low:** mostly guesswork

## Rule

Do not move a lead into Outreach Tracker unless the next manual message is obvious and non-spammy.
